About Us

Kissan Connect is a revolutionary platform connecting tenant farmers, landowners, and consumers. Our mission is to eliminate intermediaries, ensuring farmers benefit directly from their hard work while providing fresh, locally sourced produce to consumers. Through transparency and sustainability, we aim to transform agriculture.

Features

  • Tenant Farmer Registration and Profile Management
  • Landowner Hiring Tools with Secure Agreements
  • Direct-to-Consumer Sales Marketplace
  • Geolocation for Farm Accessibility
  • Blockchain-Powered Transparent Agreements
  • Insights on Sales and Market Trends

Target Market

Kissan Connect serves a diverse audience:

  • Tenant Farmers: Gain visibility and access to reliable land leasing opportunities.
  • Landowners: Find skilled farmers to maximize land productivity with secure agreements.
  • Urban Consumers: Access fresh, locally sourced produce directly from farms.
